About

Abundance recommends the optimal crops to plant for small-scale gardeners based on season, location, and historical weather data. We created this website in response to the many issues that agriculture faces today, such as food insecurity, water shortages, food waste, etc. This website draws from Google Earth Engine satellite climate data to estimate the rainfall and temperature for a given zipcode. Click here to view the JavaScript code used to compile the Google Earth Engine data used by the project. Then Abundance gives a ranked list of the thirty most common home garden crops based on their optimal growing conditions.
